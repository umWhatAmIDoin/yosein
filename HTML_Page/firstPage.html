table / tr / td Creates table. td is each square components, and tr is horizontal groups of td. (in this case, 3 td horizontally together as one tr group) Just look the table I made here, ez lul
thead / tbody Tag thead is heading (top placed components on table), while tbody tag is body (any components in table excluding thead). N/A
colspan / rowspan in td, merges the specific amount of td besides to right(for colspan) / below(for rowspan).

"td colspan=4" on td A means merging 3 td placed on right side of td A, and exist as one td A square.

"td rowspan=2" on td B means merging 1 td placed on below side of td B, and exist as one td B square.
form Creates a simple general form. Can even send to mail by using "action="mailto:email@example.com"". N/A
